Recipe for a Simple Fried Rice

Made by Shine Thao

This recipe isn't really a recipe, but something I made using the ingredients I had.

Equipment

  • 1 Small Bowl
  • Fork
  • Utensil For Stirring (Ladle or Spatula. If you're using a nonstick pan, never use metal utensils on the pan.)
  • Wide Pan (Preferably a Wok)
  • serving plate
  • Knife
  • Spoon

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 small bowl of rice
  • Oil
  • Sriracha Sauce
  • Hoisin Sauce (or Soy Sauce)
  • Any leftover meat (ex:chicken, pork, beef, or fish)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper (or White Pepper)
  • Spring Onions(Optional)
  • 1 Clove of Garlic (Optional)

Steps

  1. Crack 3 eggs into a small bowl
  2. Take whatever leftover meat you have and cut in into small bite sized pieces. (Maybe about an inch in size)
  3. (Optional)Dice a clove of garlic
  4. (Optional)Slice some spring onions
  5. Whisk the eggs using a fork until there is no snotty white visible. The mixture should just look yellow.
  6. Heat a pan on a medium heat and pour in oil.
  7. Season the eggs with salt and pepper and mix it in.
  8. (Optional) Add garlic to the pan and brown it
  9. Pour the egg mixture into the hot pan and start scrambling with your mixing utensil.
  10. When the egg starts to solidify, add in the meat protein to the pan.
  11. Stir the egg and meat for 10 seconds to warm up the meat and add the rice to the pan.
  12. Start bashing, pressing, and working the rice to separate the clumps of rice into individual grains.
  13. Once the rice is mostly separated, add in your Hoisin (or Soy) Sauce and mix until there is no white color left.
  14. Then, add in as much Sriracha as you'd like. The more Sriracha, the hotter it will be.
  15. Finally, plate up the rice.
  16. (Optional) Garnish with some spring onions.
  17. Eat the Fried Rice
